About Us

Scoop Scoop Hooray was opened in 1985 by the current owners, the Smith family, maternal grandmother. Growing up on a farm she often helped care for the cows and participated in the milking process where she learned a lot about the creamery process and spent time off the farm in the kitchen experimenting with ice cream recipes... the rest is history! We hope you enjoy our ice cream!
